! Calibration notice. Price bands reflect typical mid-2026 US used-market asking ranges (drawn from Edmunds, CarGurus, Cox/Manheim and KBB) and still vary with mileage, condition, trim and the local LA market — they're a guide, not a quote. California registration uses a Vehicle License Fee (~0.65% of value) plus fees that change; insurance for a young LA driver varies hugely by record and ZIP, so pull real quotes. Smog: gas/hybrid cars generally need a check every 2 years, but vehicles 8 model years old or newer are exempt; EVs are exempt entirely. Note federal EV tax credits ended in late 2025, and the solo-driver carpool decal depends on state reauthorization — confirm current status. Always verify with the CA DMV and a licensed insurer.
